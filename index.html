About

Jacques is the managing director of Lagari Consulting, a private company he founded in 2017.

Jacques proudly proclaims himself to be a technology geek, always keen to try out and experiment with the newest technologies. This has allowed him to develop a large and diverse skillset across many different areas.

He has a keen interest in development and cloud computing. His experience with cloud-based resources extends beyond the usual Microsoft Azure, AWS, and Google Cloud, to also include Linode and DigitalOcean.

His cybersecurity is focused mainly on penetration testing and bug bounty hunting has become a driving force behind him constantly testing the websites and applications he develops to ensure they are as secure as possible.
